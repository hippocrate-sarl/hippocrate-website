Our software solutions

Product published by Hippocrate

Sigmund

Sigmund is the first online practice management software for psychiatrists, psychotherapists and psychologists in Luxembourg.

From first contact to invoicing, including communication with the CNS, Sigmund is the ideal solution for mental health professionals who want to be freed from repetitive, time-consuming administrative tasks.
