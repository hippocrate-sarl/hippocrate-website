This is the English translation of our personal data policy. Only the French version of this policy is legally binding.

Scope

The hippocrate.lu website only collects personal data through its contact form. In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), this policy explains what data we collect, on what legal basis, for what purpose, how long we keep it, with whom we share it, and what your rights are.

The Sigmund software, published by Hippocrate and available at sigmund.lu, has its own, more detailed privacy policy, which notably covers the data of our clients and their patients.

Data controller

The controller for the personal data collected on this site is Hippocrate Sàrl, a limited liability company under Luxembourg law, registered under number B282221 in the trade and companies register.

Its registered office is located at 11 rue des Aubépines, L-8052 Bertrange (Luxembourg), and you can contact us by email at privacy@hippocrate.lu.

Data collected through the contact form

When you use the form available on our Contact us page, we collect your name, email address, the subject and content of your message, and, if you provide it, your phone number. Your name, email address, subject and message are required so that we can process your request and reply to you; the phone number is optional.

On the legal basis of our legitimate interest in responding to requests addressed to us, we process this data solely to get back to you about your message. We retain this data for three years from the date you send it, or three years after our last exchange with you. You have the right to access, rectify and erase all or part of this data; these rights can be exercised by contacting us using the means indicated at the end of this policy.

Data processor: Brevo

To receive, store and process the messages submitted through this website's contact form, we use the services of the French company Brevo SAS.

Sharing your personal data

Other than with Brevo, the data processor mentioned above, we do not share or disclose your personal data. This includes selling, exchanging, or transferring it for free or for payment. We may be required to share your data if compelled to do so by a court order.

Complaint

If you believe that the processing of your personal data by Hippocrate Sàrl constitutes a breach of the GDPR, you may file a complaint with the CNPD.
