The team behind Sigmund

Hippocrate Sàrl was founded in 2023 by three partners based in Luxembourg, with a simple mission: give carers back the time to provide better care. Sigmund, our first piece of software, was born from that observation.

Sylvain Perez

Co-founder & developer

Founder of Sigmund, Sylvain created the software in 2013 to meet the practice-management needs of his psychotherapist wife. Today, he listens closely to the whole community to continuously improve the application. He also heads Q-Leap.
